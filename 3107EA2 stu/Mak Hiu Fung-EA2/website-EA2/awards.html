Awards

Since her debut in July 2008, G.E.M. (Gloria Tang) has received over 300 music awards across the Greater China region, making her the first underage female artist to win individual awards in the music scene. In 2013, she became the youngest Hong Kong female singer to be nominated for Best Mandarin Female Singer at the 24th Golden Melody Awards.

In 2014, G.E.M.'s popularity soared after her appearance on Hunan TV's I Am a Singer, earning her the Kids' Choice Award for Most Popular Asian Artist from Nickelodeon, making her the first Hong Kong artist to receive this honor. That same year, she received three nominations at the World Music Awards. In 2016, she won the MTV Europe Music Award for Best Artist from Mainland China and Hong Kong/Taiwan.

On November 19, 2018, G.E.M. was ranked 34th on the BBC's 100 Women list, being the only Chinese woman featured. In 2020, her seventh studio album, Animal Zoo, won the Jury's Award at the 31st Golden Melody Awards, making her the sixth recipient of this award since 2007.
