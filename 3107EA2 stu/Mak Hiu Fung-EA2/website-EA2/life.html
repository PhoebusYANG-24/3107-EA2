Relationships

Before her debut, G.E.M. (Gloria Tang) was in a relationship with Bryon Kwan. In December 2010, she was spotted on a date with Taiwanese singer Yoga Lin, marking the public revelation of their relationship.

The couple maintained a low profile regarding their relationship, often described as a semi-public secret, and rumors of their breakup circulated. On March 5, 2014, Yoga Lin confirmed in a media interview that he would be taking a break from music and hinted at their separation.

On March 2, 2016, reports emerged that G.E.M. had attended a birthday party for singer Hua Chenyu in Beijing on February 6, suggesting a new romance, though neither confirmed it.

In 2018, G.E.M. publicly confirmed her relationship with Mark Ngai by sharing a photo of them at NASA on Instagram. However, by the end of 2021, Hong Kong media reported that they had quietly separated. Yet, in July 2022, Mark appeared in her new album, and they were seen together as a couple. On May 13, 2023, G.E.M. and Mark, along with their team, vacationed in the Maldives.
