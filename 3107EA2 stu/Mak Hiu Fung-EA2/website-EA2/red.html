Urban Decay

Congratulations to G.E.M. (Gloria Tang) on becoming the global ambassador for Urban Decay! She is bold and avant-garde, constantly pushing boundaries and expressing diverse perspectives through her music, just as UD boldly proclaims: "When it comes to beauty, WE ARE PRETTY DIFFERENT!" As music meets edgy makeup, watch her showcase her skills with the popular Naked eyeshadow palette series, making a statement to express her unique self!
